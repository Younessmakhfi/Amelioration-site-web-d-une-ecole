Selection criteria

The aim of the selection process is to help select the most capable, effective and suited students. Depending on the selected Track and Learning Program, the selection will be based on different criteria.

Basic Learning Program

For the admission to the Basic Learning Program, the selection is based on the CV, a motivational letter and the submission of a short project proposal (about half page). The student’s application is followed by a face-to-face interview with the School Director or the Track Directors.

Company Internship Program

For the admission to the Company Internship Program, applicants are required to submit, in addition to the requested documentation for the admission to the Basic Learning Program, a project proposal on one of the Track Projects of interest of the candidate (between half and one page).

Research Program

For the admission to the Research Program, applicants are required to submit, in addition to the requested documentation for the admission to the Basic Learning Program, a project proposal on one of the Track Projects of interest of the candidate (between half and one page).
